You can move your character with the arrow keys but, a lot of computer gamers like to use W A S D.
If you use the W A S D keys with your left hand you can use the mouse with your right hand at the same time.

You can run by holding down the shift key as you are moving but, be careful!
When you are running your character can get hung up trying to get into tight spaces.
When trying to fit into a tight space be sure to walk and try to slide or wiggle into the space.
